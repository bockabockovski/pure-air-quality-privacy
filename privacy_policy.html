Privacy Policy

Pure Air Quality does not collect or store personal user data.

Location

If the user enables location access, the app uses the device location only to display local air quality data. The location is not stored or shared.

Air Quality Data

The application retrieves environmental air quality data from the Open-Meteo Air Quality API.

Notifications

The app may display AQI notifications locally on the device.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy you may contact the developer.
